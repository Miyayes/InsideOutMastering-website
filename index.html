About (current) Approach Engineer Submission Info

Inside Out is an audio engineering service specializing in audio mastering.

Perfect your individual musical pieces, LPs and EPs.

Our Philosophy

A proper master should make a mastering engineer proud as much as it should make the artist proud of their music.

Behind every mastering session at Inside Out is a goal to maximize the potential of every song that is being mastered. A balanced blend of consistent processing for an album and individual attention to specific songs is essential here. Lots of attention is dedicated to making sure that pieces are optimized, as well as creating cohesion between them when they belong the same collection (i.e. an album or EP).

Submissions

Individual .wav files of your songs, preferably at a bit size of 24 bits and a sample rate of 44.1KHz. Ideally, unmastered mixes should not reach or exceed 0 dBFS (i.e., they should not clip). Recommended: Your mixes should not peak higher than 4db below 0db (in other words: leave at least +4db of headroom).

What you receive

Fully mastered, individual .wav files at a sample rate of 44.1 KHz. Bit sizes vary depending on where audio is to be distributed. Let Inside Out Mastering know whether mastered songs should have a bit size of 16 bits (CD) or 24 bits (digital/online distribution).
